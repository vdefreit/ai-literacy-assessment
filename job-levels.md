# Twilio Job Levels

## S1 - Junior Specialist
**Knowledge & Application:** Learns job skills and company rules to complete simple tasks. Follows instructions closely and applies basic concepts.

**Complexity & Problem Solving:** Works on regular, simple tasks using set instructions and guidelines.

**Collaboration & Interaction:** Normally gets detailed instructions for all tasks and asks questions. Works well in a team.

**Years of Experience:** No experience needed.

## S2 - Specialist
**Knowledge & Application:** Uses learned job skills and company processes to complete assigned tasks. Begins to learn more complex skills.

**Complexity & Problem Solving:** Works on tasks that are mostly regular or routine, sometimes with minor variations.

**Collaboration & Interaction:** Normally follows company procedures on routine problems and communicates with team members.

**Years of Experience:** Typically requires a minimum of 1 year of related experience.

## S3 - Intermediate Specialist
**Knowledge & Application:** Understands the job well and uses knowledge and company processes with competence. Applies skills to more varied tasks.

**Complexity & Problem Solving:** Works on tasks that are moderately difficult, sometimes requiring judgment and problem-solving.

**Collaboration & Interaction:** Normally works independently on daily work, relying on past experience and team guidance.

**Years of Experience:** Typically requires a minimum of 2 years of related experience.

## S4 - Senior Specialist
**Knowledge & Application:** As a skilled specialist, completes tasks in regular areas independently and assists others.

**Complexity & Problem Solving:** Works on tasks that require good judgment and some innovation.

**Collaboration & Interaction:** Decides methods and procedures on new tasks. Meets with colleagues and shares knowledge.

**Years of Experience:** Typically requires a minimum of 4 years of related experience.

## S5 - Lead Specialist
**Knowledge & Application:** As a highly skilled specialist, contributes to improving work processes and provides advice to others.

**Complexity & Problem Solving:** Works on difficult tasks on their own and often on interdisciplinary projects.

**Collaboration & Interaction:** Acts independently to determine methods and procedures. Works with broader teams and external contacts.

**Years of Experience:** Typically requires a minimum of 6 years of related experience.

## P1 - Associate Professional
**Knowledge & Application:** Learning basic job skills as part of a team. Learns company goals and policies.

**Complexity & Problem Solving:** Works on problems of limited scope. Completes discrete tasks.

**Collaboration & Interaction:** Forms steady working relationships with teammates and managers. Participates in team activities.

**Years of Experience:** No professional experience needed.

## P2 - Professional
**Knowledge & Application:** Has basic knowledge and growing skills. Uses standard procedures and company tools.

**Complexity & Problem Solving:** Addresses moderately challenging but routine problems. Solves typical issues.

**Collaboration & Interaction:** Builds productive relationships to solve shared problems within the team.

**Years of Experience:** Typically requires a minimum of 2 years of related experience.

## P3 - Senior Professional
**Knowledge & Application:** Has solid, fully developed skills and uses the common knowledge base well. Applies company policies and industry best practices.

**Complexity & Problem Solving:** Uses evaluation and analysis to solve problems and improve processes.

**Collaboration & Interaction:** Builds networks, sometimes outside team. Adjusts approach based on feedback.

**Years of Experience:** Typically requires a minimum of 5 years of related experience.

## P4 - Staff Professional
**Knowledge & Application:** Has advanced skill proficiency and deep subject matter knowledge and expertise. Solves complex problems and improves processes. Uses knowledge from related areas to develop new ideas. Often mentors others and is important in cross-team projects.

**Complexity & Problem Solving:** Addresses complex issues that require a deep dive. Exercises judgment in adapting methods. Trusted to deliver complete work.

**Collaboration & Interaction:** Connects with key colleagues and often advises on complex topics. May have ownership for specific business goals as part of a team.

**Years of Experience:** Typically requires a minimum of 8 years of related experience.

## P5 - Principal Professional
**Knowledge & Application:** Known as an expert with deep specialization and recognized for innovation and complex problem-solving.

**Complexity & Problem Solving:** Solves unique or ambiguous challenges, using creative approaches and collaboration.

**Collaboration & Interaction:** Known as a thought leader who coordinates across groups. Acts as an internal consultant.

**Years of Experience:** Typically requires a minimum of 12 years of related experience.

## P6 - Senior Principal Professional
**Knowledge & Application:** Serves as a visionary technical leader whose expertise drives significant advancements.

**Complexity & Problem Solving:** Work and decisions influence entire programs and organizational direction.

**Collaboration & Interaction:** Exercises wide latitude in determining objectives and approaches. Frequently interacts with leadership.

**Years of Experience:** Typically requires a minimum of 15 years of related experience.

## P7* - Distinguished Professional
**Knowledge & Application:** Applies deep technical expertise in current and emerging technologies to guide innovation.

**Complexity & Problem Solving:** Works with abstract ideas and complex, intangible concepts.

**Collaboration & Interaction:** Oversees engineering research and advanced projects influencing broader strategy.

**Years of Experience:** Typically requires a minimum of 15 years of related experience.

## M2 - Junior Manager
**Knowledge & Application:** Leads with clear objectives and follows defined company policies and procedures.

**Complexity & Problem Solving:** Looks at information to solve routine problems and improve workflows.

**Collaboration & Interaction:** Manages routine operational plans and helps members of the team. Explains and interprets technical information clearly. Supervises skilled individual contributors or teams.

**Years of Experience:** Varies

## M3 - Manager
**Knowledge & Application:** Makes decisions about resources and sets how to meet goals within the team.

**Complexity & Problem Solving:** Solves a wide range of problems and considers consequences carefully.

**Collaboration & Interaction:** Translates plans into action and manages activities, schedules, and resources. Collaborates with leaders and key contacts outside immediate teams. Manages multiple teams through supervisors, handling medium-level complexity.

**Years of Experience:** Varies

## M4 - Senior Manager
**Knowledge & Application:** Sets goals for larger areas of the company and defines overall direction and expectations.

**Complexity & Problem Solving:** Handles difficult and ambiguous problems, often involving multiple functions and stakeholders.

**Collaboration & Interaction:** Advises and persuades senior leaders and outside contacts. Oversees multiple disciplines or departments; responsible for significant operational and strategic outcomes.

**Years of Experience:** Varies

## M5 - Director
**Knowledge & Application:** Collaborates with senior leaders to define strategy and aligns departmental efforts toward company goals.

**Complexity & Problem Solving:** Navigates undefined problems with big-picture thinking and innovation.

**Collaboration & Interaction:** Influences long-term results (up to five years or more). Regularly negotiates and influences at the executive level. Directs the activities of functions within a division or large department.

**Years of Experience:** Varies

## M6 - Senior Director
**Knowledge & Application:** Creates the vision and strategy that affects a significant portion of the company or business.

**Complexity & Problem Solving:** Leads with strategic thinking, developing appropriate responses to broad challenges.

**Collaboration & Interaction:** Influences board-level and external leadership decisions. Manages small to medium teams or departments focused on complex or strategic initiatives.

**Years of Experience:** Varies

## E7 - Vice President
**Knowledge & Application:** Is an expert in their own business area and industry; recognized internally and externally.

**Complexity & Problem Solving:** Considers impact on the company at large. Leads complex, cross-functional efforts.

**Collaboration & Interaction:** Translates company strategy into practical 2-5 year plans. Partners with other leaders to solve complex, enterprise-wide problems. Manages departments. Usually oversees directors.

**Years of Experience:** Varies

## E8 - Senior Vice President
**Knowledge & Application:** Demonstrates expert knowledge of their corporate domain and industry; frequently cited as a thought leader.

**Complexity & Problem Solving:** Influences industry trends and drives innovation.

**Collaboration & Interaction:** Partners with other leaders to develop solutions for enterprise challenges. Leads an area/sub function or a business line. Oversees multiple departments or large scale strategic initiatives.

**Years of Experience:** Varies
